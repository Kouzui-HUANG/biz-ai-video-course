## 聲學工程與混音模組 (Acoustic Engineering & Mixing Module) [知識增強]
*此區塊決定音色質感 (Timbre) 與空間物理 (Physics)。*

### A. 樂器與音色紋理 (Detailed Timbral Texture)
* **Synthetic**: Sawtooth (鋸齒波), Square Wave, FM Synth, 808 Bass, Reese Bass, Arpeggiator.
* **Guitar/Strings**: Stratocaster (Clean/Funk), Les Paul (Distortion), Pedal Steel, Palm Muted, Tapping.
* **Keys/Organic**: Felt Piano (柔音鋼琴), Rhodes, Wurlitzer, Hammond B3, Harpsichord, Marimba.
* **Percussion**: 909 Drums, Acoustic Kit (Brushes), Breakbeat, Taiko, Congas, Glitch Percussion.

### B. 混音特效與後製 (Mixing & Post-Production FX)
* **Dynamic Shaping**: Sidechain Compression (抽吸感), Multiband Compression (多頻段壓縮), Noise Gate (斷奏).
* **Textural Color**: Lo-Fi, Vinyl Crackle (黑膠底噪), Tape Saturation (磁帶飽和), Bitcrusher (位元破碎), Tube Warmth (管機溫暖).
* **Spatial Audio**: Wide Stereo, Dry (貼耳), Wet (濕潤), Plate Reverb, Shimmer Reverb (閃爍混響), Reverse Reverb, Binaural.
* **Frequency**: High-Pass Filter (電話音), Scooped Mids (V型EQ), Bright, Dark.
