## 全息流派層級矩陣 (Holographic Genre Hierarchy Matrix)
*基礎骨架：定位核心風格與年代特徵。*

### A. 搖滾、金屬與龐克 (Rock/Metal/Punk)
* **Timeline**: 50s Rockabilly, 70s Prog, 80s Post-Punk, 90s Grunge, 00s Math Rock/Midwest Emo.
* **Sub-genres**: Shoegaze (瞪鞋), Blackgaze, Djent, Industrial Metal, Art Rock, Post-Hardcore.

### B. 電子、舞曲與實驗 (Electronic/IDM/Experimental)
* **Club**: Techno (Acid/Dub), House (Deep/Tech/Lo-Fi), DnB (Liquid/Neurofunk), Trance.
* **Bass & Future**: Future Bass, Dubstep, Trap, Wave, Hyperpop, Glitch Hop.
* **Experimental**: IDM (Intelligent Dance Music), Deconstructed Club, Musique Concrète, Drone, Ambient.

### C. 都會、嘻哈與靈魂 (Urban/Hip-Hop/Soul)
* **Roots**: Neo-Soul, Jazz Rap, Funk, Motown, Blues.
* **Modern**: Drill, Trap, Cloud Rap, Alternative R&B, Trip-Hop.

### D. 古典、爵士與世界 (Classical/Jazz/World)
* **Tradition**: Baroque, Romantic Era, Bebop, Swing, Bossanova, Flamenco, Gamelan.
* **Modern Fusion**: Nu-Jazz, Modern Classical, Cinematic Hybrid.
