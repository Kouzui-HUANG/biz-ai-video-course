# Analysis Framework — Detailed Reference

## A. Photography & Composition (攝影與構圖)

| Dimension       | What to Identify                                                                 |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Lens Distance   | Extreme close-up, Close-up, Medium close-up, Medium shot, Full shot, Wide shot, Establishing shot |
| Camera Angle    | Eye-level, Low angle, High angle, Dutch angle/tilt, Bird's eye, Worm's eye      |
| Focal Length    | 24mm (wide), 35mm (standard wide), 50mm (normal), 85mm (portrait), 135mm (telephoto), 200mm+ (super telephoto) |
| Depth of Field  | Shallow DoF / Bokeh (f/1.4–f/2.8), Medium DoF (f/4–f/8), Deep focus (f/11+)    |
| Composition     | Rule of thirds, Golden ratio, Leading lines, Symmetrical, Centered, Frame within frame, Diagonal |
| Camera Sim      | Leica M10, Sony A7R IV, Fujifilm X-T5, Hasselblad X2D, Canon EOS R5, iPhone portrait mode |

## B. Art Style & Medium (藝術風格與媒介)

### Medium / Material
Oil painting, Watercolor, Gouache, Acrylic, Charcoal, Pencil sketch, Ink wash, Digital art, 3D render, Photograph, Polaroid, Film grain, Collage, Mixed media

### Era / Movement
Renaissance, Baroque, Impressionism, Art Nouveau, Art Deco, Surrealism, Pop Art, Minimalism, Cyberpunk, Steampunk, Vaporwave, 90s anime cel, Y2K aesthetic, Cottagecore, Dark academia

### Render Engine (if CG/3D)
Octane Render, Unreal Engine 5, Blender Cycles, Cinema 4D, V-Ray, Arnold, KeyShot

### Brushwork & Texture
Impasto (heavy texture), Flat color, Soft airbrush, Matte finish, Glossy/wet look, Grainy/Noise, Halftone dots, Cross-hatching, Stippling

## C. Subject Details (主體特徵)

### Demographics
- **Apparent ethnicity**: East Asian, Southeast Asian, South Asian, Middle Eastern, Northern European, Southern European, African, Latin American, Mixed heritage
- **Apparent age range**: Infant, Child, Teenager, Young adult (20s), Adult (30s-40s), Middle-aged (50s), Elderly
- **Gender presentation**: Masculine, Feminine, Androgynous, Non-binary

### Hair
- **Style**: Straight, Wavy, Curly, Coily, Bob, Pixie cut, Ponytail, Twin-tails, Updo, Braids, Loose, Messy, Slicked back
- **Length**: Cropped, Short, Shoulder-length, Mid-back, Waist-length
- **Color**: Jet black, Dark brown, Chestnut, Auburn, Platinum blonde, Ash blonde, Silver/grey, Vivid (red, blue, pink, ombre, highlights)

### Attire
- **Type**: Casual, Formal, Streetwear, Uniform, Traditional/cultural, Fantasy, Futuristic, Athletic
- **Fabric**: Cotton, Silk, Leather, Denim, Lace, Wool, Satin, Mesh, Synthetic
- **Accessories**: Jewelry, Glasses, Hats, Scarves, Bags, Watches, Piercings, Tattoos

### Expression & Kinesics
- **Micro-expressions**: Genuine smile (Duchenne), Smirk, Pensive, Melancholic, Defiant, Serene, Surprised, Fearful, Contemptuous
- **Gaze**: Direct eye contact, Averted, Downcast, Looking off-camera, Over-the-shoulder
- **Pose**: Static/relaxed, Dynamic/action, Contrapposto, Leaning, Crouching, Arms crossed, Hands in pockets, Mid-gesture

## D. Scene & Atmosphere (場景與氛圍)

### Environment
- **Indoor**: Studio, Living room, Café, Office, Library, Workshop, Bathroom, Kitchen, Corridor, Bedroom
- **Outdoor**: Street, Rooftop, Forest, Beach, Mountain, Garden, Urban alley, Field, Desert, Waterfront
- **Fantasy/Sci-fi**: Floating islands, Neon-lit cityscape, Ancient ruins, Spaceship interior, Underwater, Void/abstract

### Time of Day
Golden hour, Blue hour, Sunrise, Harsh midday sun, Overcast afternoon, Dusk/twilight, Midnight, Pre-dawn

### Lighting
| Type               | Description                                     |
|--------------------|-------------------------------------------------|
| Natural            | Sunlight, Window light, Overcast diffusion       |
| Artificial         | Neon, Fluorescent, Candlelight, Spotlight, LED   |
| Volumetric         | God rays, Light shafts through fog/dust          |
| Rim / Back light   | Silhouette edge glow, Halo effect                |
| Chiaroscuro        | High contrast light/shadow (Caravaggio style)    |
| Rembrandt          | Triangle of light on cheek                       |
| Butterfly / Glamour| Overhead frontal, shadow under nose              |
| Split              | Half-face illumination                           |

### Mood / Atmosphere
Ethereal, Melancholic, Nostalgic, Euphoric, Serene, Tense, Ominous, Whimsical, Cinematic, Dreamy, Gritty, Warm/cozy, Cold/clinical, Mysterious, Romantic
