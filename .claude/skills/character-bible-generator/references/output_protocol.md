# Output Protocol: Character Bible

Use the exact Markdown structure below for the final output (Phase 2). All headers must be preserved exactly as shown, without deletion.

### 階段二：《人物設定聖經》正式文稿

#### A. 人物總覽 (Character Executive Summary)
*(150-200 words. Precisely summarize the character's "soul contour", first impression, and core internal tension.)*

#### B. 細節資料 (High-Resolution Character Data)

**1. 實體建構 (Physical Entity)**
* **姓名**：[Full Name/Nickname/Alias]
* **基本參數**：[Age] / [Gender] / [Height] / [Weight]
* **面部與微表情**：[Eye characteristics, facial features, and micro-expressions under specific emotions]
* **外顯風格**：[Hairstyle / Daily clothing style and signature accessories, reflecting their psychological state]

**2. 心理與性格矩陣 (Psychological Matrix)**
* **性格素描**：[3-5 core adjectives, with behavioral manifestations in specific situations]
* **人格標籤**：[MBTI + customized subtype explanation] / [Zodiac] / [Enneagram or Blood type]
* **價值觀與底線**：[Things they fiercely defend / Triggers that cause rage or a breakdown]

**3. 社會與文化根基 (Socio-Cultural Roots)**
* **身分背景**：[Nationality/Ethnicity/Beliefs and impact of values]
* **原生家庭**：[Family structure and specific impact on personality/trauma]
* **語言能力**：[Native language/Fluency/Specific accents or class-based linguistic traits]

**4. 語言與行為特徵 (Linguistic & Behavioral Fingerprint)**
* **對話口吻**：[e.g., Cynical and sharp, or gentle but distant]
* **慣用語癖**：[3-5 commonly used words, tone particles, or specific sentence structures]
* **潛意識動作**：[Unconscious body language when anxious, lying, or highly focused]

**5. 專業軌跡 (Professional Trajectory)**
* **職涯背景**：[Current position and core past experiences that shaped their abilities]
* **能力邊界**：[Professional strengths and fatal blind spots]
* **溝通受眾 (TA)**：[Groups they resonate with most easily, and groups they clash with most frequently]

**6. 隱藏維度 (Hidden Dimensions)**
* **不為人知的秘密**：[Habits or past events known only to themselves or very few people]
* **發展潛力與毀滅隱患**：[Key nodes in future stories that could lead to their sublimation or downfall]
