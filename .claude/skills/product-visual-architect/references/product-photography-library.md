## 1. Principle 1 — Commanding the Light

Name a definite source AND quality. Never "good lighting" or mixed/uncertain light.

### Core sources
- `soft, diffused studio lighting` — large softbox, even, shadow-free; default for clean hero shots
- `dramatic side-lighting (raking light) to emphasize surface texture` — reveals grain, weave, embossing
- `warm morning window light` / `golden-hour backlight` — lifestyle warmth, long soft shadows
- `cinematic rim lighting / edge light` — bright outline separating product from a dark ground
- `crisp top-down key light with a single soft shadow` — flat-lay clarity
- `high-key even illumination` (bright, low-contrast) vs `low-key chiaroscuro` (dark, moody, single light)
- `gradient light falling off across a seamless backdrop` — premium studio depth
- `dappled light through foliage` — natural lifestyle texture

### Specular control (critical for shiny goods)
- `controlled specular highlights along the high edges` — glass, metal, glossy plastic
- `soft gradient reflection on the polished surface` — avoids blown-out hotspots
- `large soft reflection panel mirrored in the glossy face` — phones, lacquer, chrome
- `matte diffusion to suppress harsh glare` — for satin/matte finishes

## 2. Principle 2 — Defining the Environment

Decide the background. Two families:

### Studio (hero / packshot)
- `seamless pure-white background (#ffffff), e-commerce ready` — marketplace main image
- `neutral gray backdrop, color code #f0f0f0` — premium neutral
- `minimalist monochrome set, soft floor-to-wall curve (cyclorama)`
- `product resting on a subtle reflective acrylic surface` — mirror-foot reflection
- `tonal backdrop matching the product's accent color` — brand-cohesive
- `floating product on a solid color field with a soft contact shadow`

### Lifestyle (in-context) — keep it CLEAN and PLAUSIBLE
- `shallow depth of field, beautiful creamy bokeh background`
- `on a rustic dark-wood table` / `on warm honed marble` / `on a raw concrete slab`
- `uncluttered modern kitchen counter, softly out of focus`
- `linen-draped surface with one or two restrained props`
- `sunlit minimalist interior, blurred plants in the background`
- Rule: at most 1–3 supporting props, never a busy scene; the product stays unmistakably the hero.

### Surfaces & micro-staging
`brushed slate`, `travertine`, `pebbled leather mat`, `rippled water surface`, `dry stone with moss`, `silk fabric folds`, `crushed velvet`, `fresh ice / water droplets` (beverages, cosmetics).

## 3. Principle 3 — Locking the Composition

Direct the camera: angle + framing + composition rule.

### Angle
`eye-level` · `slight high angle (~30°)` · `dynamic low-angle (hero/heroic)` · `straight overhead (90° flat-lay)` · `three-quarter front (45°) hero angle` · `dutch tilt (energetic)`.

### Framing / distance
`full-product packshot` · `medium product shot` · `tight crop` · `extreme close-up (macro shot)` · `floating/levitation shot` · `group/range lay-down`.

### Composition rules
`rule of thirds` · `centered symmetrical composition` · `elegant use of negative space` · `leading lines toward the logo` · `diagonal dynamic composition` · `tight grid flat-lay (knolling)`.

### Lens cues (natural-language)
`shot on an 85mm macro for compression and detail` · `35mm for honest lifestyle perspective` · `100mm macro for extreme detail` · `f/1.8 for shallow depth` · `f/11 for full-product sharpness`.

---

## 4. Principle 4 — Emphasizing Focus & Texture (per-material)

Always pair a clarity grade with the ACTUAL material being resolved.

### Clarity grades
`hyper-realistic, 4K photorealistic` · `tack-sharp focus on [hero feature/logo]` · `razor-sharp product, softly blurred background` · `every micro-detail crisply resolved`.

### Material → texture phrase
| Material | Use this phrasing |
|----------|-------------------|
| **Leather** | `intricate detail of the hand-stitched leather seams, visible grain and supple sheen` |
| **Fabric / apparel** | `capturing the woven texture of the fabric, individual fibers and weave pattern visible` |
| **Knitwear** | `chunky knit loops and yarn texture rendered in crisp detail` |
| **Metal / chrome** | `brushed-metal micro-grain with controlled specular streaks` / `polished mirror chrome` |
| **Glass / crystal** | `crystal-clear glass with refraction and clean caustic highlights` |
| **Plastic (matte)** | `smooth matte polymer with a soft-touch finish, no cheap-plastic glare` |
| **Ceramic / porcelain** | `glazed ceramic with a subtle glossy sheen and smooth curvature` |
| **Wood** | `natural wood grain and warm matte finish` |
| **Paper / packaging** | `crisp printed cardstock, sharp edges, subtle paper texture and spot-UV gloss` |
| **Liquid / cosmetic** | `glossy serum texture with light refraction, dewy droplets on the surface` |
| **Food** | `fresh, appetizing texture with natural moisture and crumb/sear detail` |
| **Jewelry / gem** | `faceted gemstone brilliance with fire and sparkle, micro-pavé detail` |
| **Electronics** | `seamless unibody finish, precise chamfered edges, fingerprint-free surface` |

## 5. Principle 5 — Controlling Color & Aesthetics

Set fidelity + palette + mood/style.

### Fidelity
`accurate color reproduction` · `true-to-life color fidelity` · `brand-accurate color matching`.

### Palette / mood
- `warm and inviting color palette` — food, home, cozy lifestyle
- `cool, clean, clinical palette` — tech, skincare-science, medical
- `desaturated, moody aesthetic` — premium, editorial, masculine
- `vibrant high-saturation pop` — youthful, playful, FMCG
- `soft pastel palette` — beauty, baby, wellness
- `monochrome tonal palette` — luxury minimalism

### Overall style
`clean professional commercial photography` · `high-end editorial product photography` · `minimalist Scandinavian aesthetic` · `luxury advertising still life` · `bright airy e-commerce style` · `dark moody chiaroscuro still life`.

## 6. Shot-Type Portfolio

Each entry lists its principle defaults. Build the trio by combining contrasting entries.

| Shot type (名稱) | Light | Environment | Composition | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Classic Hero / Studio (經典主圖)** | soft diffused studio | seamless white / #f0f0f0 | eye-level or 3/4 hero, centered, negative space | marketplace main image |
| **Lifestyle / In-context (情境氛圍)** | warm window / golden hour | clean real surface, bokeh | rule of thirds, 35mm | brand storytelling, ads |
| **Macro Detail (微距細節)** | dramatic raking side-light | dark or tonal, fully blurred | extreme close-up, 100mm macro | texture, craftsmanship, logo |
| **Scale / In-hand (比例展示)** | soft daylight | minimal context | eye-level medium, hand holding | size reassurance |
| **Overhead Flat-lay (俯視平鋪)** | crisp top-down key | flat surface + props | 90° overhead, knolling/thirds | sets, ranges, kits, food |
| **Floating / Levitation (懸浮動態)** | studio + rim light | solid color field | low/eye-level, dynamic, negative space | energetic FMCG, tech |
| **Group / Range (家族合影)** | soft even studio | seamless or tonal | staggered depth, leading lines | line-ups, variants, gift sets |

Default trio when category is unclear: **Classic Hero · Lifestyle · Macro Detail** (maximum coverage contrast).

## 7. Recommended Trios by Product Category

- **Apparel / bags / shoes** → Hero (on neutral) · Lifestyle (worn/staged) · Macro (stitching & material)
- **Cosmetics / skincare** → Hero (clean clinical) · Macro (glossy texture/droplets) · Lifestyle (vanity/bathroom light)
- **Food & beverage** → Lifestyle (table scene, steam/condensation) · Macro (fresh texture) · Hero/Flat-lay (ingredients knolling)
- **Tech / gadgets** → Hero (floating studio) · Macro (chamfer/port detail) · Lifestyle (desk in use)
- **Jewelry / watches** → Macro (gem brilliance) · Hero (dark luxury still life) · Scale/In-hand (worn on wrist)
- **Furniture / home** → Lifestyle (room context) · Hero (3/4 on seamless) · Macro (material/joinery)
- **Beverage bottles / cans** → Hero (rim-lit on reflective acrylic, ice/droplets) · Lifestyle (poured, in scene) · Macro (label & condensation)
- **Packaged FMCG** → Hero (e-commerce white) · Floating (dynamic) · Flat-lay (range/family)

## 8. Worked Example

**Input:** "A handcrafted brown leather minimalist wallet."

````markdown
**提案 1：經典主圖 / Classic Hero Shot**

**English Prompt:**
```text
A handcrafted brown leather minimalist wallet photographed under soft, diffused studio lighting from a large overhead softbox, resting on a seamless pure-white background (#ffffff) with a faint soft contact shadow. Eye-level three-quarter hero angle with the wallet positioned using the rule of thirds and elegant negative space to its right. Hyper-realistic 4K photorealistic rendering, tack-sharp focus capturing the supple full-grain leather, its natural grain and the precise hand-stitched edge seams. Accurate color reproduction of the rich cognac-brown tone, clean professional commercial e-commerce photography, bright and premium.
```

**中文翻譯：**
一只手工棕色皮革極簡錢包，在大型頂部柔光箱的柔和擴散棚光下拍攝，置於無縫純白背景（#ffffff）上並帶有淡淡的柔和接觸陰影。平視四分之三的主角視角，以三分法構圖並在右側保留優雅留白。超高擬真 4K 照片級渲染，絕對銳利的對焦捕捉柔軟的全粒面皮革、其天然紋理與精準的手縫邊線。準確還原濃郁干邑棕色調，乾淨專業的電商商業攝影，明亮而高級。

---

**提案 2：情境氛圍 / Lifestyle Mood**

**English Prompt:**
```text
The same brown leather wallet lying on a rustic dark-wood table bathed in warm morning window light raking from the left, casting long soft shadows. Shallow depth of field with a beautiful creamy bokeh of a blurred café interior behind it, one out-of-focus coffee cup as a restrained prop. Shot at 35mm, eye-level, rule-of-thirds composition. Hyper-realistic, razor-sharp on the leather grain and stitching while the background melts away. Warm and inviting color palette, high-end editorial lifestyle photography.
```

**中文翻譯：**
同一只棕色皮革錢包置於鄉村風深色木桌上，沐浴在自左側斜射的溫暖晨間窗光中，投下長而柔和的陰影。淺景深搭配模糊咖啡館內景的美麗奶油散景，一只失焦的咖啡杯作為克制的陪襯道具。以 35mm 平視拍攝，三分法構圖。超高擬真，皮革紋理與縫線銳利清晰，背景則柔化消融。溫暖宜人的色調，高端編輯風格的生活情境攝影。

---

**提案 3：微距細節 / Macro Detail**

**English Prompt:**
```text
Extreme close-up macro shot of the brown leather wallet's stitched corner, lit by dramatic side-lighting (raking light) that emphasizes every fiber of the texture against a dark tonal background that falls fully out of focus. Shot on a 100mm macro lens, tight diagonal composition. Hyper-realistic 4K, tack-sharp intricate detail of the hand-stitched leather seams, visible full-grain pores and the supple sheen of the leather. Desaturated moody aesthetic with accurate cognac-brown color, luxury advertising still life.
```

**中文翻譯：**
棕色皮革錢包縫線轉角的極端微距特寫，以強調紋理每一根纖維的戲劇性側光（斜射光）打亮，背景為完全失焦的深色調。以 100mm 微距鏡頭拍攝，緊湊的對角線構圖。超高擬真 4K，絕對銳利地呈現手縫皮革接縫的精緻細節、清晰可見的全粒面毛孔與皮革柔潤的光澤。低飽和、有情緒的風格，準確的干邑棕色，奢華廣告靜物。
````
