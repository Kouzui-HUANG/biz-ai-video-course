## 1. 要領一 —— 指揮光線

指名確定的光源「與」光質。絕不寫「good lighting」或混合／不確定的光線。

### 核心光源
- `soft, diffused studio lighting` —— 大型柔光箱、均勻、無陰影；乾淨主圖的預設
- `dramatic side-lighting (raking light) to emphasize surface texture` —— 顯現紋路、編織、壓紋
- `warm morning window light` / `golden-hour backlight` —— 情境的溫暖、長而柔的陰影
- `cinematic rim lighting / edge light` —— 將產品從深色底分離的明亮輪廓
- `crisp top-down key light with a single soft shadow` —— 平鋪清晰度
- `high-key even illumination`（明亮、低對比）vs `low-key chiaroscuro`（暗、有情緒、單光）
- `gradient light falling off across a seamless backdrop` —— 高級棚拍縱深感
- `dappled light through foliage` —— 自然情境的紋理感

### 高光控制（亮面商品關鍵）
- `controlled specular highlights along the high edges` —— 玻璃、金屬、亮面塑料
- `soft gradient reflection on the polished surface` —— 避免過曝熱點
- `large soft reflection panel mirrored in the glossy face` —— 手機、烤漆、鍍鉻
- `matte diffusion to suppress harsh glare` —— 適用於緞面／霧面處理

## 2. 要領二 —— 定義環境

決定背景。兩大家族：

### 棚拍（主圖／packshot）
- `seamless pure-white background (#ffffff), e-commerce ready` —— 電商主圖
- `neutral gray backdrop, color code #f0f0f0` —— 高級中性
- `minimalist monochrome set, soft floor-to-wall curve (cyclorama)`
- `product resting on a subtle reflective acrylic surface` —— 鏡面倒影
- `tonal backdrop matching the product's accent color` —— 品牌色協調
- `floating product on a solid color field with a soft contact shadow`

### 情境（生活場景）—— 保持「乾淨」且「合理」
- `shallow depth of field, beautiful creamy bokeh background`
- `on a rustic dark-wood table` / `on warm honed marble` / `on a raw concrete slab`
- `uncluttered modern kitchen counter, softly out of focus`
- `linen-draped surface with one or two restrained props`
- `sunlit minimalist interior, blurred plants in the background`
- 法則：最多 1–3 件陪襯道具，絕不雜亂的場景；產品始終是無可爭議的主角。

### 表面與微佈景
`brushed slate`、`travertine`、`pebbled leather mat`、`rippled water surface`、`dry stone with moss`、`silk fabric folds`、`crushed velvet`、`fresh ice / water droplets`（飲品、彩妝）。

## 3. 要領三 —— 鎖定構圖

指揮鏡頭：視角 + 取景 + 構圖法則。

### 視角
`eye-level` · `slight high angle (~30°)` · `dynamic low-angle (hero/heroic)` · `straight overhead (90° flat-lay)` · `three-quarter front (45°) hero angle` · `dutch tilt (energetic)`。

### 取景／距離
`full-product packshot` · `medium product shot` · `tight crop` · `extreme close-up (macro shot)` · `floating/levitation shot` · `group/range lay-down`。

### 構圖法則
`rule of thirds` · `centered symmetrical composition` · `elegant use of negative space` · `leading lines toward the logo` · `diagonal dynamic composition` · `tight grid flat-lay (knolling)`。

### 鏡頭提示（自然語言）
`shot on an 85mm macro for compression and detail` · `35mm for honest lifestyle perspective` · `100mm macro for extreme detail` · `f/1.8 for shallow depth` · `f/11 for full-product sharpness`。

## 4. 要領四 —— 強調焦點與質感（依材質）

務必將清晰度等級與「實際被解析的材質」搭配。

### 清晰度等級
`hyper-realistic, 4K photorealistic` · `tack-sharp focus on [hero feature/logo]` · `razor-sharp product, softly blurred background` · `every micro-detail crisply resolved`。

### 材質 → 質感用語
| 材質 | 使用此用語 |
|------|------------|
| **皮革 Leather** | `intricate detail of the hand-stitched leather seams, visible grain and supple sheen` |
| **布料／服飾 Fabric** | `capturing the woven texture of the fabric, individual fibers and weave pattern visible` |
| **針織 Knitwear** | `chunky knit loops and yarn texture rendered in crisp detail` |
| **金屬／鍍鉻 Metal** | `brushed-metal micro-grain with controlled specular streaks` / `polished mirror chrome` |
| **玻璃／水晶 Glass** | `crystal-clear glass with refraction and clean caustic highlights` |
| **塑料（霧面）Plastic** | `smooth matte polymer with a soft-touch finish, no cheap-plastic glare` |
| **陶瓷／瓷器 Ceramic** | `glazed ceramic with a subtle glossy sheen and smooth curvature` |
| **木材 Wood** | `natural wood grain and warm matte finish` |
| **紙／包裝 Paper** | `crisp printed cardstock, sharp edges, subtle paper texture and spot-UV gloss` |
| **液體／化妝品 Liquid** | `glossy serum texture with light refraction, dewy droplets on the surface` |
| **食品 Food** | `fresh, appetizing texture with natural moisture and crumb/sear detail` |
| **珠寶／寶石 Jewelry** | `faceted gemstone brilliance with fire and sparkle, micro-pavé detail` |
| **電子產品 Electronics** | `seamless unibody finish, precise chamfered edges, fingerprint-free surface` |

## 5. 要領五 —— 控制色彩與美學

設定 色彩準確度 + 色調 + 氛圍／風格。

### 色彩準確度
`accurate color reproduction` · `true-to-life color fidelity` · `brand-accurate color matching`。

### 色調／氛圍
- `warm and inviting color palette` —— 食品、居家、溫馨情境
- `cool, clean, clinical palette` —— 3C、護膚科學、醫療
- `desaturated, moody aesthetic` —— 高級、編輯感、陽剛
- `vibrant high-saturation pop` —— 年輕、活潑、快消品（FMCG）
- `soft pastel palette` —— 美妝、嬰幼、保健
- `monochrome tonal palette` —— 奢華極簡

### 整體風格
`clean professional commercial photography` · `high-end editorial product photography` · `minimalist Scandinavian aesthetic` · `luxury advertising still life` · `bright airy e-commerce style` · `dark moody chiaroscuro still life`。

## 6. 鏡頭組合（Shot-Type Portfolio）

每個條目列出其要領預設。以對比的條目組合出三案。

| 鏡頭類型（名稱） | 光線 | 環境 | 構圖 | 最適用於 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **經典主圖／棚拍（Classic Hero / Studio）** | soft diffused studio | seamless white / #f0f0f0 | eye-level 或 3/4 主角、置中、留白 | 電商主圖 |
| **情境／生活場景（Lifestyle）** | warm window / golden hour | 乾淨真實表面、bokeh | rule of thirds、35mm | 品牌敘事、廣告 |
| **微距細節（Macro Detail）** | dramatic raking side-light | 深色或同調、全模糊 | extreme close-up、100mm macro | 質感、工藝、logo |
| **比例／手持（Scale / In-hand）** | soft daylight | 極簡情境 | eye-level medium、手持 | 尺寸安心感 |
| **俯視平鋪（Overhead Flat-lay）** | crisp top-down key | 平面 + 道具 | 90° 俯視、knolling/三分法 | 套組、系列、組合包、食品 |
| **懸浮動態（Floating / Levitation）** | studio + rim light | 純色場 | 低／平視、動態、留白 | 動感快消、3C |
| **家族合影／系列（Group / Range）** | soft even studio | seamless 或同調 | 錯落縱深、引導線 | 產品線、變體、禮盒組 |

品類不明時的預設三案：**經典主圖 · 情境 · 微距細節**（涵蓋面對比最大）。

---

## 7. 各品類推薦三案組合

- **服飾／包款／鞋履** → 主圖（中性背景）· 情境（穿著／佈景）· 微距（縫線與材質）
- **彩妝／護膚** → 主圖（乾淨臨床感）· 微距（亮面質感／水珠）· 情境（梳妝台／浴室光）
- **食品飲料** → 情境（餐桌場景、蒸氣／凝水）· 微距（新鮮質感）· 主圖／平鋪（食材 knolling）
- **3C／數位產品** → 主圖（懸浮棚拍）· 微距（倒角／接口細節）· 情境（桌面使用中）
- **珠寶／腕錶** → 微距（寶石火光）· 主圖（深色奢華靜物）· 比例／手持（配戴於腕）
- **家具／家居** → 情境（房間情境）· 主圖（3/4 無縫背景）· 微距（材質／榫接）
- **瓶裝飲料／罐裝** → 主圖（反光壓克力上的輪廓光、冰塊／水珠）· 情境（傾倒、入景）· 微距（標籤與凝水）
- **包裝快消品（FMCG）** → 主圖（電商白底）· 懸浮（動感）· 平鋪（系列／家族）

## 8. 完整範例

**輸入：** 「一只手工棕色皮革極簡錢包。」

````markdown
**提案 1：經典主圖 / Classic Hero Shot**

**English Prompt:**
```text
A handcrafted brown leather minimalist wallet photographed under soft, diffused studio lighting from a large overhead softbox, resting on a seamless pure-white background (#ffffff) with a faint soft contact shadow. Eye-level three-quarter hero angle with the wallet positioned using the rule of thirds and elegant negative space to its right. Hyper-realistic 4K photorealistic rendering, tack-sharp focus capturing the supple full-grain leather, its natural grain and the precise hand-stitched edge seams. Accurate color reproduction of the rich cognac-brown tone, clean professional commercial e-commerce photography, bright and premium.
```

**中文翻譯：**
一只手工棕色皮革極簡錢包，在大型頂部柔光箱的柔和擴散棚光下拍攝，置於無縫純白背景（#ffffff）上並帶有淡淡的柔和接觸陰影。平視四分之三的主角視角，以三分法構圖並在右側保留優雅留白。超高擬真 4K 照片級渲染，絕對銳利的對焦捕捉柔軟的全粒面皮革、其天然紋理與精準的手縫邊線。準確還原濃郁干邑棕色調，乾淨專業的電商商業攝影，明亮而高級。

---

**提案 2：情境氛圍 / Lifestyle Mood**

**English Prompt:**
```text
The same brown leather wallet lying on a rustic dark-wood table bathed in warm morning window light raking from the left, casting long soft shadows. Shallow depth of field with a beautiful creamy bokeh of a blurred café interior behind it, one out-of-focus coffee cup as a restrained prop. Shot at 35mm, eye-level, rule-of-thirds composition. Hyper-realistic, razor-sharp on the leather grain and stitching while the background melts away. Warm and inviting color palette, high-end editorial lifestyle photography.
```

**中文翻譯：**
同一只棕色皮革錢包置於鄉村風深色木桌上，沐浴在自左側斜射的溫暖晨間窗光中，投下長而柔和的陰影。淺景深搭配模糊咖啡館內景的美麗奶油散景，一只失焦的咖啡杯作為克制的陪襯道具。以 35mm 平視拍攝，三分法構圖。超高擬真，皮革紋理與縫線銳利清晰，背景則柔化消融。溫暖宜人的色調，高端編輯風格的生活情境攝影。

---

**提案 3：微距細節 / Macro Detail**

**English Prompt:**
```text
Extreme close-up macro shot of the brown leather wallet's stitched corner, lit by dramatic side-lighting (raking light) that emphasizes every fiber of the texture against a dark tonal background that falls fully out of focus. Shot on a 100mm macro lens, tight diagonal composition. Hyper-realistic 4K, tack-sharp intricate detail of the hand-stitched leather seams, visible full-grain pores and the supple sheen of the leather. Desaturated moody aesthetic with accurate cognac-brown color, luxury advertising still life.
```

**中文翻譯：**
棕色皮革錢包縫線轉角的極端微距特寫，以強調紋理每一根纖維的戲劇性側光（斜射光）打亮，背景為完全失焦的深色調。以 100mm 微距鏡頭拍攝，緊湊的對角線構圖。超高擬真 4K，絕對銳利地呈現手縫皮革接縫的精緻細節、清晰可見的全粒面毛孔與皮革柔潤的光澤。低飽和、有情緒的風格，準確的干邑棕色，奢華廣告靜物。
````
